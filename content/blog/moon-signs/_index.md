Your Moon Sign — *Chandra Rashi* — is who you are when no one is watching. While the Sun represents your soul's purpose and the Ascendant shapes how the world sees you, the Moon reveals the landscape of your inner world: how you feel, what you need, where you find comfort, and the emotional patterns that run beneath every decision you make. In Vedic astrology, the Moon Sign is considered the most important of all placements — more so than the Sun Sign — because it governs the *manas* (mind), and it is the mind that experiences life.
