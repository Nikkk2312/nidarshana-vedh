Your Ascendant — *Lagna* — is the mask and the mission. It is the sign that was rising on the eastern horizon at the exact moment of your birth, and in Vedic astrology, it is considered the single most important point in the entire chart. The Lagna sets the stage for everything: it determines which houses rule which areas of your life, it colours how you approach the world, and it defines the physical vessel through which your Sun and Moon must express themselves. If the Sun is who you are and the Moon is how you feel, the Ascendant is how you *arrive*.
