The Houses — *Bhāvas* — are the stage upon which the planetary drama of your life unfolds. While signs describe *how* a planet behaves and nakshatras reveal *why*, the houses tell you *where* — in which arena of life a planet channels its energy. The 1st house is the self, the 7th is the other, the 10th is your mark upon the world, and the 12th is where the world dissolves. Every planet placed in a house brings its own agenda, its own gifts, and its own karmic lessons to that domain.
